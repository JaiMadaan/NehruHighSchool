About Us

Welcome to Nehru High School, a beacon of educational excellence for over 47 illustrious years! Situated in the heart of our vibrant community, Nehru High School has been a cornerstone of academic achievement, shaping young minds and nurturing future leaders since its inception. At Nehru High School, we take immense pride in our commitment to providing quality education from nursery through 10th grade. Our holistic approach to learning goes beyond textbooks, fostering a well-rounded educational experience that encompasses academic rigor, extracurricular activities, and character development. Recognized for our unwavering dedication to student success, Nehru High School boasts a team of experienced educators who are passionate about inspiring and empowering every child to reach their full potential. Our dynamic curriculum is designed to stimulate intellectual curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and instill a lifelong love for learning. One of the hallmarks of Nehru High School is our affordable fee structure, ensuring that quality education remains accessible to all members of our community. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to receive a top-notch education without financial barriers, and we are committed to making this a reality. Beyond academics, Nehru High School is a vibrant hub of activity, where students have the opportunity to explore their interests and talents through a wide array of extracurricular programs, including sports, arts, music, and community service initiatives. Through these diverse experiences, students develop essential life skills, build lasting friendships, and cultivate a sense of responsibility to the world around them. As we embark on our 48th year of excellence, Nehru High School remains steadfast in our mission to nurture young minds, foster creativity, and prepare students to thrive in an ever-changing world. We invite you to join our esteemed school community and embark on an enriching educational journey that will lay the foundation for a bright and promising future. Welcome to Nehru High School, where every child is valued, supported, and empowered to succeed.

Teacher Educational Certificates

The teachers at our school are dedicated professionals who bring expertise, passion, and commitment to their roles every day. They strive to create lifelong learners who are prepared to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
